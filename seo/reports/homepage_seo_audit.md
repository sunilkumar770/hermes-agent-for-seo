## 2. Heading Hierarchy

* **H1 (Main Heading)**: `Rent smarter. Own less.`
  * *Assessment*: Strong brand slogan.
  * *SEO Recommendation*: Add a secondary, keyword-focused span or subtitle to make the H1 more informative to crawlers (e.g., `Rent smarter. Own less. P2P Rental Marketplace in India`).
* **Sub-headings (H2/H3)**:
  * *Category headings*: DSLR Cameras, Premium Bikes, Verified Cars, Event Equipment are marked up.
  * *SEO Recommendation*: Ensure H2 tags are used explicitly for main sections (e.g., "Popular Rentals in Hyderabad", "How Peer-to-Peer Renting Works") to give search crawlers clear section contexts.
